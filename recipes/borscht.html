Borscht

Description

Borscht is a variety of beetroot stew found in Eastern Europe. Thought to have originated in Ukraine, it offers both sweet and savory flavor profiles, making it a great addition to your culinary repertoire.

  • Prep Time: 25 min
  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Yield: 8 servings
