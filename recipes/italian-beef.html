Italian beef

Description

Italian beef is a type of roast beef sandwich that originated in Chicago. With its spicy and savory flavor profile, it is sure not to disappoint for any occasion.

  • Prep Time: 20 min
  • Cook Time:8-10 hours
  • Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast
  • Giardiniera
  • Pepperoncini
  • Italian seasoning
  • Provolone
  • Sub buns

Directions

  1. Let the meat rest at room temperature. Salt the beef.

  2. Sear the beef, then transfer it to the slow cooker.

  3. Deglaze the pan with beef broth, then add the liquid to the slow cooker.

  4. Sprinkle the Italian seasoning over the beef.

  5. Add peppers and giardiniera.

  6. Cook on low for 8 to 10 hours, then shred the beef. Cook for another 30 minutes after shredding.
